your mission: Guide Ariel through 8 challenging worlds to obtain the magic orb which contains the power of growth, fire and flight in order to reach and defeat the tempest Bowser and rescue the mute princess Aria. Good luck shakespear! 

HINT - There are two routes to reach the princess, one is the mute version of her and one is not.

TIP - While playing this game you may discover that one of the worlds is more challenging than the rest. It is possible to warp past it if you are clever enough to find the hidden vine.

Caviet: If you happen to hit a vine block while you are fully powered it will not fully sprout and you will not be able to climb up it.
